TJ ROGERS

TJ has been a well known street skater for a while now. 2022 has been the best year of his career for a while now.
He managed to put out two unbelievable parts while also beating cancer this year.

TJ started the year off with an Es Footwear part titled "T.J.I.F".
This part features TJ taking his technical abilities to massive spots. As far as ability goes, this one may be the most advanced part of the year.

TJ's second part of the year was also for Es Footwear.
"The Unstoppable TJ Rogers" was shot entirely on a VX1000 which gives the part the feel of a classic technical ledge part.
