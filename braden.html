BRADEN HOBAN

Get familiar with Braden Hoban because you’re going to be hearing his name a lot. Braden has destroyed every spot in his path and he’s only getting started.

Braden started the year off hot with his part in Emerica's "Emerge" video.
Braden closes the video out strong, attacking every rail and hubba in sight.

Just a month after his Emerica part, Braden had another full length part in Toy Machine Skateboards "Vaccine" video.
He put his technical abilities on full display in this part, flipping into or out of almost every grind. Braden's abilities seem limitless after watching these back to back video parts.
