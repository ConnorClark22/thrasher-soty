KADER SYLLA

Kader is at the top of the list when it comes to raw talent. At such a young age, he is on a trajectory that mirrors a young Ishod Wair.
The crazy thing is that we know this kid is just going to keep getting better, which is hard to imagine.

Kader put out a Welcome to Adidas part this year that features him attacking big rails and flipping his board down even bigger stair sets.
The part is an easy watch as his smooth flow matches perfectly to a classic hip-hop heater.
