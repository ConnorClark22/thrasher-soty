WADE DESARMO

Wade Desarmo has been a household name in skating for nearly 20 years.
His technical abilities, quick feet, and smooth style have been unmatched for a long time and in 2022, he reminded everyone why.

This year Wade had an incredible video part in Primitive Skateboards "Define" video.
At age 38, it's clear his bag of tricks hasn't shrunk at all. In fact it seems he is still progressing.
